BHSOH’s 2010-2011 Academic Year

by admin under Uncategorized

While paying respect to Thai National flage by students at BHSOH, during 2010-2011 academic year

GED class

This year the numbers of the students are as many as last year. There are over 300 students who have been in the list of the school.  Then, we all teachers are ready to pass through this exciting year with the highest energy and to share our experiences, knowledge and skills with them.  We are very proud to be their teachers and, of course, to closely guide them to the point that they would rather get in the future.
