Uploading your site ----------------------------------------------------------

Uploading your site could not be simpler. You can either use an FTP program or FrontPage (On Request). Please do NOT use both ( this can cause accounts to become corrupted). If you choose to upload using an FTP program you should use the following details: Address / Host: (ftp.yourdomain.com) Username: (your account username) Password (your account password). To upload your files without the need for client software simply visit: http://webftp.yourdomain.com

Once you have started your FTP session, you must upload your files to the main_html folder.

Administer your site ---------------------------------------------------------

To manage your web sites configuration we provide you with our unique web based control panel. To access this you simply need to visit: https://www.yourdomain.com:8443/client/

Check your mail online ------------------------------------------------------

In addition to checking your mail offline, you can also check it online! Every Visual Netcentre account comes with the popular web mail system - SquirrelMail. This allows you to read your mail from any computer connected to the Internet. To access Webmail you simply need to visit: http://webmail.yourdomain.com

Administer your database ---------------------------------------------------

Every Beatsuite account comes complete with a pre installed MySQL database. To administer this database we provide the popular PHPMyAdmin as standard. To access this you simply need to visit: http://webmysql.yourdomain.com

Check your web site statistics -----------------------------------------------

To keep track of your web sites visitors and their activity every account is pre-installed with the webalizer traffic analysis software. To access this you simply need to visit: http://www.yourdomain.com/webstat
