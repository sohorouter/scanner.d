Toddle About

All you need for your baby & toddler


Welcome to Toddle About! The only resource you need if you have children aged bump to 5 in the Northampton, Daventry, Towcester and Brackley areas.

There is so much going on for little ones in Northamptonshire, but it is surprisingly hard to find out what, where and when. Which is why we brought out Toddle About! Whether you are looking for baby groups in Northampton, toddler groups in Towcester or children's shops in Daventry, you will find them in our online directory.

We've also made it easy for you to find baby and toddler events and great Northamptonshire family days out in our events section, which you can order by date, or search by location.
