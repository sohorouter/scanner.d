Color Climax Corporation was founded in 1966 as a clandestine black-and-white pornographic magazine, since the ban on pornography in Denmark was to be abolished in 1968. In 1969 the first colour issue of Color Climax was published. Color Climax Corporation became a major supplier of pornography to the continent in the early 1970s because in this period production of pornography was still illegal in most other European countries.

In the 1970s, Color Climax began to produce pornographic films. These were 8 mm loops. Because of their length, availability and lack of elaborate plots, they differed from standard pornographic feature films of the period. By 1980s, video tapes were released, sometimes as compilations of loops. Color Climax films were mostly in line of the chic pornography of the era with stylised surroundings, women wearing garter stockings, etc. However, Color Climax films usually had a wider range of contents including She-males and Watersports. By 2004 Color Climax had many popular european filmstars such as Anna Marek, Tove Jensen, Rocco Siffredi, Tiffany Towers and John Holmes in their archives on their website.
