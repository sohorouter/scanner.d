Vision

Headache-free 3D TV

emotion3D facilitates the creation and displaying of video content on 3D screens by leveraging its proprietary technology for converting between different 3D video formats.

Our vision is to empower content creators to add new sensations to the viewing experience and bring 3D into the mainstream, from movie theatres to advertising, exhibitions, visualization and home cinema.

We offer a high-quality service to convert stereoscopic video content to suit different 3D display input formats.
