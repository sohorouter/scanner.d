ABOUT MEMFIS:

MEMFIS is a Framework Programme 7 project funded by the European Commission. The project was launched in September 2008 and it is going to run for 3 years.

The MEMFIS project consortium will aim at achieving a major technological leap by developing a miniaturised, widely practically applicable FT-spectrometer module by making use of various new and emerging technologies, in particular in the field of (optical) micro-systems technology.

 

MEMFIS AT A GLANCE:

  • Mission: to demonstrate the feasibility of a spectroscopic mid-infrared analyser based on miniature FT-IR spectrometers.
