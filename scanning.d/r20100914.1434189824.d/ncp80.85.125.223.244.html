Bespoke Yoga Lessons | Workshops | Retreats


espritrelax offers bespoke yoga lessons, workshops and retreats in the South of France to encourage the integration of body, mind and spirit.

The techniques presented are based on Thai Yoga and the Dynamic Yoga learning method, an invigorating yoga practice that brings you to the peak of your physical and mental powers by integrating:
  • yoga postures
  • breathing exercises
  • attention & concentration
  • meditation
  • and healthy living
... safely, effectively, and satisfyingly.

Everyone can benefit from these techniques, regardless of their level of flexibility, strength, stamina or age.

Exercises are adapted to meet each practitioner's needs and comforts. The only thing that is required is to be open to that which is actually happening in the present moment. Our concept >>
