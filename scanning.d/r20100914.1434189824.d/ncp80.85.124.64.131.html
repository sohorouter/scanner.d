Stratodesk is the new LISCON

Klagenfurt, 18. 8. 2010 - Newly founded Stratodesk Software GmbH presents itself as successor of Thin Client vendor LISCON. The company is founded and owned by Emanuel Pirker, an IT entrepreneur who had also founded and operated LISCON.

"Our customers and partners won't notice much difference", CEO Emanuel Pirker, says. "Team, office location, salespeople and contact information stay the same".

Stratodesk will continue to develop current products and deliver absolutely new solutions - more interesting announcements will follow in autumn. Of course Stratodesk offers to continue existing maintenance/subscription contracts and of course start new subscriptions.

"Our influence on the Central European market has been huge in the past. We'll have it like this in the future as well", Emanuel Pirker states in confidence. "Furthermore, there some real innovations to come, much more than our current portfolio. We look forward to work together with our customers and partners with great excitement."

Stratodesk Software GmbH, Kohldorfer Strasse 98, 9020 Klagenfurt, Austria
