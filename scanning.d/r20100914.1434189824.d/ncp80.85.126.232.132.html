Welcome to the RunningBall Academy!

This portal of the RunningBall Academy is dedicated to all Scouts working for RunningBall Sports Information (www.rball.com) – the World’s leading institution for real-time sports information and to everyone who is applying for becoming a Scout.

The RunningBall Academy Website is designed to help the Scouts with almost everything in relation to their job. It is crucial for keeping and improving the high standard of reporting RunningBall is known for.

RunningBall Scouts attend sport events and collect data and statistics live from the place of action using the PDA (Personal Digital Assistant).

On this page all Scouts are provided the necessary training and receive all updates on the rules of Scouting for RunningBall.

If you want to apply for being a Scout and a member of our team, please fill in the contact form on the right.
