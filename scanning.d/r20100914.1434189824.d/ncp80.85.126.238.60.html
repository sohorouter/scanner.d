Welcome to my homepage

I am research assistant at the "Institute of Software Technology and Interactive Systems" (ISIS) of Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien). I have my Bachelor From Kerman University, Iran in Mathematics applied in Computer Science. Afterward I have worked as Software Manager, Network Manage and Chief Technology Officer till 2003 when I joined ISIS as project assistant. As project assistant I have been involved in some European and National research projects.

I have received my master degrees in "Software Engineering and Internet Computing" and "Information Management" from Vienna University of Technology. I have finished my Ph.D. in Business Informatics in 2009 and I am now perusing a second Ph.D. degree in Architecture & Building Science.

My research areas include Semantic Web, Knowledge management, Web 2.0, Internet Computing, and Building Information Modeling.
